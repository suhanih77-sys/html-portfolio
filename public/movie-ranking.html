The Best Movies According to Suhani

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Me Before You

Me Before You is a 2016 British romantic drama film directed by Thea Sharrock.

How to Lose a Guy in 10 Days

An advertising executive and ladies' man, to win a big campaign,
bets that he can make a woman fall in love with him in 10 days.

Notting Hill

A set of circumstances makes Anna Scott, a famous actress,
fall in love with William Thacker, owner of a bookstore in Notting Hill.
